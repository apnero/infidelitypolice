Adultery Statistics

Documenting statistics of deaths, marriages, divorces and births is an on-going process for federal and state agencies. These events are easily monitored, evaluated and analyzed later on. To the contrary accurate records on infidelity are not being tracked and can not be accomplished. Accurately obtaining data for research of adulterous activities, its' causes, background and scope, is impossible.

About the Affair:
