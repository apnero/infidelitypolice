D.W.I. Defense Investigations:
D.W.I. arrests are on the rise. "The only open and shut DUI cases are the ones where no defense is mounted". An investigation can determine if an individual was wrongly arrested and charged. We can determine whether or not probable cause even existed for the motor vehicle stop; without probable cause for the stop, the evidence gathered thereafter could be suppressed. Our investigation can reveal errors and mistakes made by the arresting officer, medical personnel, or other individuals related to the case. Although there is a national trend of equipping police vehicles with in-car cameras, very few officers realize that the videotape is discoverable. Officers become immune to the fact that the camera is even there. It is quite likely that our investigation will show inconsistencies between what the officer put in his report and what the videotape had recorded. Christopher paoletti, private investigator has made hundreds of arrests including D.W.I. arrests. He was trained and certified in Breathalyzer Operation and more importantly the Horizontal Gaze Nystagmus. He was also trained and certified to administer the Standardized Field Sobriety Tests. Christopher Paoletti believes that "there is no other crime where more innocent people are arrested and convicted than there are in DUI/DWI prosecutions". Often times the arresting officer's reports depict a different sequence of events than the in-car video camera had recorded.

Our investigation may uncover reasonable doubt sufficient to defend your case and win an acquittal. We will provide you with a detailed report that will dramatically support your defense against an improper D.W.I. arrest. What better way to build a defense against an improper D.W.I. arrest than to utilize the services of a retired police officer? There is none!
