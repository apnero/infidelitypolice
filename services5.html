How Does it Work?
The live GPS is a vehicle tracking system which uses Global Positioning Satellites (GPS). The GPS satellite signals are continuously broadcasting from an orbit of 20,000 miles and the live GPS tracker receives those signals to calculate its location. The longitude, latitude, Unit ID and other GPS information is transmitted to the tracking facility and you access it via the internet.

Power is supplied by direct connecting the unit to the vehicle's power or an its own internal battery.
